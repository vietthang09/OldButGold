About OBG

OBG is a marketplace where people can purchase, sell, swap, and donate unwanted products. All you have to do is provide us with some details and photos about the item you no longer need, and we'll take care of the rest. Our goal is to encourage people to live a more frugal lifestyle and to minimize waste as much as possible.
